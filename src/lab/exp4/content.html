Pseudorandom Generators and CPA Security
Introduction

RC4 is a popular algorithm for generating a stream of random bits given a small random seed (usually derived from the secrat key) as input. Inthis experiment we shall familiarize ourselves with RC4 and design an encryption scheme that is secure against ciphertext-only attack

Theory

For a very brief introduction to RC4 algorithm, click here


Objective

To learn about the RC4 algorithm and use it to design an encryption scheme that is secure against eavesdropping (ciphertext-only attack) but not secure against stronger attacks like chosen-plaintext attack and chosen-ciphertext attack
